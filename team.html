President/CEO

Eliza Meeks

Born in a small village in Germany, Eliza began her apprenticeship making pocket watches at a young age. Under her Grandfather's tutelage, Eliza became the youngest master pocket watch maker at the age of 9. Her elite skills allowed her to quickly rise though the ranks, revolutionizing the design teams of multiple companies she has worked for, leading to her founding Supreme Watches in 2012.

Design Lead

Madeline Power

A hard charging go getter. Madeline began her career as a freelance logo and t-shirt designer. Through her designing a line of t-shirts featuring cats wearing watches, her talents caught the eye of Eliza. Her impeccable design tastes and natural leadership abilities have reshaped our design team and allowed us to be considered the leader in implementing new technology into the perfect meld of classic and cutting edge.

Human Resources Manager

Andrew Rock

Andrew joins us after a career of managing circus acts. After a lifestyle change to ethical veganism and rapidly changing views on animal cruelty, he began seeking a new career path. Finding inspiration from his favorite scene in P.F., he began collecting pocket watches. His new love for pocket watches led him to a watch makers expo, where a conversation about Bill Nye and Neil Degrasse Tyson, became an impromtu interveiw with Eliza.

Procuct Coordinator

Willie hates watches and initially felt like he was a horrible fit for the position in the company, but his ability to control the the chaotic flow between departments has been a boon. He also brings a flair to the office incorporating morale events, though some question his interest in elves and wearing of elf ears on a regular basis. Eliza welcomes the distractions and mirth.
